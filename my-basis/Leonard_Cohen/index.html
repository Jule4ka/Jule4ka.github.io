    Nevermind


    The war was lost
    The treaty signed
    I was not caught
    I crossed the line

    I was not caught
    Though many tried
    I live among you
    Well disguised

    I had to leave
    My life behind
    I dug some graves
    You'll never find

    The story's told
    With facts and lies
    I had a name
    But nevermind

    Nevermind
    Nevermind
    The war was lost
    The treaty signed

    There's truth that lives
    And truth that dies
    I don't know which
    So nevermind

    Your victory

    A record of
    Our little lives

    Our spoons our knives

    The stones we cut
    The songs we made

    Names so true
